What do we do?

Financial Markets are amazing just the way they are; They offer endless opportunities for those who can grap them, and risk for those who can calculate it, make inferences about it and prepare for the worst case.
Markets also tend to reflect the imperfect, irrational judgment and behaviour of human beings; which either means securities, derivatives or currencies (for example's sake) tend to be either underpriced or overpriced.

We, the five core members of Trading Club TBS, have come up with the idea of launching a cause to offer a high quality, low cost, and up-to-date financial markets education.
Through our main project: ESD, we will be engaged in Pear2Pear collective learning experience while trading the global financial markets with a real account and thus giving our members a real exposure to what it is really like to trade markets in action using all the financial tools available: such as Leverage, MetaTrader4 Platform, Technical Indicators, Fundamental (NEWS) Trading and so much more.

We will be bringing valuable sponsors along the way, which will enrich the experience even further.
